🌍 Global Human Migration Routes

An interactive satellite & topographic atlas of the world's major irregular migration corridors. Click a route to zoom in and explore its geography, border crossings, blockages & reroutes — and follow the journey of one traveler. Solid lines = primary routes; dashed lines = reroutes taken when the main path is blocked. Zoom in anywhere: the satellite view is real imagery, down to individual fences, harbors and trails.
